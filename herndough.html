Herndough – Bread & Pastries

Heirloom loaves, pastries & baked goods from our homestead kitchen.

Herndough is our home bakery at Herndon Homestead—country sourdough, flavor‑packed loaves, biscuits, pastries, and homestyle cakes. This is where heritage and flavor rise together.

We bake with simple ingredients, long fermentation, and time‑honored methods for better texture, deeper taste, and easier digestion. Our menu follows the seasons, and everything is meant to be shared at the table. Classes and bake‑days are coming soon—come learn, bake, and break bread with us.
