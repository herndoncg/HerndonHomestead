Who We Are

A homestead dream with both boots in the soil.

Sydney was born and raised in Snohomish, Washington before moving to Texas to attend Texas A&M University. Colton was born and raised in Longview, Texas and has always had a heart for the outdoors and ranch life, including a family ranch in South Texas.

We’re a team in the truest sense: Sydney loves to cook (and dream up new ways to nourish people). Colton loves all things outdoors — hunting, fishing, ranch projects — and also enjoys time in the kitchen. Together, we’re building a homestead where we can provide for ourselves, grow natural produce, cultivate orchards, and raise livestock with care.

Colton is pursuing a degree in Mechanical Engineering and runs a handful of businesses. Sydney is kicking off her own entrepreneurial journey while studying Animal Science with a focus on genetics. Our home is a place where faith, hard work, and hospitality meet — and everyone has a seat at the table.
